About Us

Akshamrit AI started as a simple goal to make the power of AI and LLMs available to all Businesses in an easy and affordable way.

Crafting Excellence Through Innovation and Dedication

We are passionate professionals committed to delivering exceptional results that exceed expectations and drive meaningful transformation.

Businesses and the teams who serve their customers — spend hours every day on WhatsApp, answering the same questions, tracking the same orders, sending the same updates. That's time pulled away from the work that actually grows the business. We created Akshamrit AI to solve that problem directly, We built it working in coordination with Human and AI. The goal was never to replace the personal touch that makes businesses special — it's to automate the repetitive parts of customer conversation so business owners can spend their time where it matters most.

Right now, Akshamrit AI is built for small and medium businesses. As we grow, we see the same problem — and the same opportunity — at larger scale too.
